### **Maternity Chatbot for Emotional Support**

---

**Overview**
This project involves the development of a Maternity Chatbot designed to provide emotional support for second-time pregnant women who have experienced a prior miscarriage. 
The chatbot leverages Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques to analyze user sentiments and offer appropriate responses, 
focusing on enhancing mental well-being during pregnancy.

---

**Features**

**Sentiment Analysis:** Understands and evaluates user sentiments to provide empathetic responses.

**Emotional Support:** Offers comforting and supportive dialogue tailored to the needs of pregnant women.

**Custom NLP Implementation:** Uses advanced models to ensure accuracy in understanding emotional context.

**Usage**

Access the chatbot through the deployed web interface.
Input queries or share emotions to receive supportive responses and resources.

---

**Example Interaction**

**User: "I am feeling anxious about my pregnancy."**

**Chatbot:** "I'm sorry to hear that. Pregnancy can bring a lot of emotions. Taking deep breaths and focusing on positive thoughts might help. Would you like me to suggest relaxation techniques?"
